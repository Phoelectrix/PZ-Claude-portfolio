Journalism Air Traffic Technology International September 2025

Drone Economics

Feature article exploring how environmental footprint tools are transforming the economics and business models of commercial aviation and Innovative Air Mobility.

This feature article was authored to secure coverage in key industry press as part of the dissemination strategy for the ImAFUSA and RefMap Horizon Europe projects. The article targeted professional stakeholders in the aviation and urban air mobility sectors, translating complex scientific and economic research findings into accessible industry journalism.

The piece explored the emerging field of environmental footprint assessment tools and their impact on the economics of both traditional commercial aviation and the new generation of innovative air mobility solutions including drones and urban air taxis. It examined how sustainability considerations are reshaping business models and regulatory frameworks across the sector.

As Dissemination and Communication Manager for both projects, the article demonstrated the ability to craft compelling narratives from technical research, pitch to industry publications, and successfully place features that reach targeted professional audiences.
