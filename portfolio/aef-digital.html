Digital & Editorial Argolic Environment Foundation April 2021 – August 2022

Website & Social Media Management

Launched and managed the Argolic Environment Foundation’s entire web and social media presence from scratch, building the digital infrastructure for a fledgling conservation organisation.

The Argolic Environment Foundation (AEF) was a newly established conservation organisation within the Conservation Collective network when this work began. The role involved building the organisation’s entire digital presence from the ground up.

Key responsibilities and achievements included:

  • Worked within the Conservation Collective website template to build out the AEF’s online presence
  • Produced all website content, sourced images, and designed the final layout
  • Managed the website on an ongoing basis, writing and uploading regular news updates and project descriptions
  • Developed and produced a regular newsletter to engage supporters and stakeholders
  • Established social media accounts on Facebook, Instagram, and other major platforms
  • Produced regular social media content and designed a strategy to connect with supporters and build the foundation’s following

The work demonstrated the ability to build an organisation’s communications infrastructure from zero, combining content creation, web management, and social media strategy into a coherent digital presence that supported the foundation’s conservation mission.
